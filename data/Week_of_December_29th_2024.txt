Sunday Prep for the Week of 12/29/24
4 SERVINGS
1. Pho Broth with Chicken
  
INGREDIENTS:
  2 (32 oz) boxes vegetable or chicken stock
  1 (1-inch) piece fresh ginger, bruised
  1 cinnamon stick
  3 cloves garlic, peeled and smashed
  4 cardamom pods, bruised
  15 whole black peppercorns
  1/2 teaspoon whole fennel seeds
  2 tablespoons soy sauce
  1.5 tablespoons maple syrup
  4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  1 cup water
  
DIRECTIONS:
Place all ingredients in a large pot and bring to boil.
Lower to a simmer and cook covered for 30 minutes. Let
cool and remove chicken from the pot to a plate. Strain
the soup (or use a sieve to fish out the spices) and then
shred chicken with two forks. Place chicken back in the
pot and store once cooled.
  
2. Spiced Sweet Potatoes
  
INGREDIENTS:
  6 medium sweet potatoes, cut on bias
  4 tablespoons oil
  Salt, to season
  2 teaspoons cumin
  2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  2 teaspoons garlic powder
  2 teaspoons onion powder
  
DIRECTIONS:
Preheat oven to 425° and line two (pareve) baking
sheets with parchment paper. Place sweet potatoes on
the baking sheets (in a single layer) and roast for about
60 minutes until golden and crisp. Let cool and store.
  
3. Cauliflower and Leeks
  
INGREDIENTS:
  2 heads cauliflower
  2 leeks, tops and bottoms trimmed, halved,
washed well, and cut into 2-inch pieces
  Salt, to season
  3 tablespoons oil
  
DIRECTIONS:
Place vegetables on a (pareve) baking sheet in a single
layer and coat in salt and oil. Roast at 425° for 45
minutes until golden. Let cool and store.
  
4. Peppers and Onions
5
INGREDIENTS:
  3 green or yellow peppers, thinly sliced
  1 onion, halved and thinly sliced
  Oil
  Salt
DIRECTIONS:
In a large (pareve) pan heat the oil on high heat and
sauté the peppers and onions (seasoning with salt) until
softened, 6-8 minutes. Let cool and store.
  
5. Tahini-Lime Sauce
  
INGREDIENTS:
  3 tablespoons tahini
  2 limes, juiced
  1.5 tablespoons maple syrup
  Salt, to season
  3 tablespoons water (to thin out as needed)
  
DIRECTIONS:
Whisk together all ingredients in a mason jar and store.
  
6. Moroccan Spiced Chicken
  
INGREDIENTS:
  1/4 cup light coconut milk
  3 cloves garlic, minced
  1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  1 teaspoon cumin
  1 teaspoon onion powder
  1 teaspoon salt
  1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  1 teaspoon fresh ginger, minced
  1 lemon, juiced
  6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  
DIRECTIONS:
Mix all ingredients together in a storage bag. Add the
chicken and let marinate for 30 minutes or ideally
overnight. When ready to cook: Preheat oven to 425°.
Heat an oven-safe skillet or grill with oil. Sear chicken
until golden on each side, about 3-4 minutes. Remove to
a plate and continue with remaining pieces adding more
oil as needed. Place all pieces of chicken back in the pan
and put the pan in the oven to finish cooking for an
additional 8-10 minutes until completely cooked through,
or 165° internal temperature.

 Monday Rally
Moroccan Spiced Chicken Sandwiches
with Peppers and Onions and Spiced
Sweet Potatoes
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
– Moroccan spiced chicken
– peppers and onions (half)
– tahini-lime sauce (half)
– spiced sweet potatoes (half)
Additional Ingredients:
-Sriracha, optional
-Buns
Mint, optional
DIRECTIONS :
Heat oven to 300° and warm chicken (covered), and sweet potatoes
(uncovered)for 25- 30 minutes until warmed through. Meanwhile if desired,
mix some of the tahini-lime sauce with some sriracha to create an even
more delicious sauce for your sandwich. Toast buns if you like and spread
with sauce. Load with peppers and onions, and sliced chicken. Garnish
with mint if desired and serve with sweet potatoes on the side!

 Tuesday Rally
Pho Bowls
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
– pho
– optional cauliflower and leeks
(half)
Additional Ingredients:
-Raw rice noodles, or shiritaki,
zucchini noodles, or other
noodles of choice
-Bean sprouts
-Cilantro
-Mint
-Jalapeno, optional
-Fresh limes
DIRECTIONS :
Remove soup from the fridge and bring to a low boil. Add in raw rice
noodles and cook until soft or you can cook the pasta in a separate pot.
Cook, strain, rinse and add to soup. In bowls divvy up hot broth with
noodles and top with bean sprouts, cilantro, mint, jalapeño if desired, and
optional cauliflower and leeks. Serve with fresh lime!

 Wednesday Rally
Southwestern Kale Salad with Tahini
Lime Sauce
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
– cauliflower and leeks
– spiced sweet potatoes (half)
– tahini-lime sauce (half)
Additional Ingredients:
-Kale
-Sliced avocado
-Drained and rinsed black beans
-Fresh lime
-Mint
-Cilantro
-Pomegranate seeds
-Crumbled feta cheese
DIRECTIONS :
In bowls section out kale (you can pre-massage it with some of the tahini-
lime vinaigrette or even just plain lime juice), sweet potatoes, cauliflower
and leeks, sliced avocado, drained and rinsed canned black beans, and
garnish with fresh lime, leftover mint or cilantro if desired, and
pomegranate seeds. Top with more tahini-lime vinaigrette and crumbled
feta! If you prefer the vegetables warm simply heat the cauliflower and
sweet potatoes in a 300° oven until warmed through!

 Thursday Rally
Confetti Quiche Muffins
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
– peppers and onions (half)
Additional Ingredients:
-6 eggs (or 3 eggs for 2 servings, and 9
eggs for 6 servings)
-16 oz cottage cheese (or 8 oz for 2
servings, and 24 oz for 6 servings)
-1 1/4 cups shredded mozzarella
cheese (or 2/3 cup for 2 servings, and
1 3/4 cups for 6 servings)
-4 oz vegan beef crumbles or chopped
vegan pepperoni, optional (or 2 oz for 2
servings, and 6 oz for 6 servings)
-1 teaspoon salt (or 1/2 teaspoon for 2
servings, and 1 1/2 teaspoons for 6
servings)
-1/4 teaspoon black pepper (or 1/8
teaspoon for 2 servings, and 3/8
teaspoon for 6 servings)
-1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (or 1/4
teaspoon for 2 servings, and 3/4
teaspoon for 6 servings)
DIRECTIONS :
Preheat oven to 325° and line muffin tins with papers or grease pan VERY
well. In a large bowl whisk together all ingredients. Pour into prepared
muffin pans and bake for 40 minutes, or until set. Serve in optional pitas or
tortillas with ketchup, salsa, guacamole etc. Throw together a fresh salad
or cut up vegetables to complete the meal!
