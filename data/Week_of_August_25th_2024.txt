Swap the chicken breast for thighs if preferred.
Substitute the asparagus for green beans or broccolini for a different veggie option. 
Swap the sweet potato fries with russet potato fries if preferred.
Use fresh, frozen, or canned corn for the corn tomato orzo salad.
Any combination of tender herbs can be used in the corn tomato orzo salad including mint, parsley, chives,
basil, and/or dill.

Sunday Prep for the Week of 8/25/24
4 SERVINGS
1. Sweet Potato Fries
  
INGREDIENTS:
  4 large sweet potatoes
  2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  Pinch salt and pepper
  
DIRECTIONS:
Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit and line two
large baking sheets (or one large one) with parchment
paper. Toss the sweet potatoes with the olive oil and a
generous pinch of salt and pepper until evenly coated.
Spread the sweet potatoes evenly between the baking
sheets and roast for about 20-25 minutes or until tender
and golden brown. Broil on high for 1-3 minutes, if
desired for further browning. Let the potatoes cool, then
cover and refrigerate.
  
2. Sauteed Asparagus
  
INGREDIENTS:
  2 bunches asparagus
  2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  Pinch salt and pepper
  
DIRECTIONS:
Heat a large skillet over medium high heat and add the
olive oil. When the oil is hot, add the asparagus and a
generous pinch of salt and pepper. Saute over medium
high heat for about 7-10 minutes or until tender and
browned. Let the asparagus cool, then cover and
refrigerate.
  
3. Lemony Orzo
  
INGREDIENTS:
  1 pound orzo pasta
  1 lemon, juiced and zested
  2 tablespoons olive oil
  Pinch salt and pepper
  
DIRECTIONS:
Cook the pasta according to package directions, until just
al dente. Reserve half of the orzo for the corn tomato
orzo salad. Toss the remaining half with the lemon juice
and zest, olive oil, and a generous pinch of salt and
pepper. Once cooled, cover and refrigerate lemony orzo.
  
4. Summer Corn Tomato Orzo Salad
  
INGREDIENTS:
  1/2 pound cooked orzo
  2 cups frozen corn, thawed
  1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  
DIRECTIONS:
In a large bowl, toss together all ingredients until well
combined. Cover and refrigerate.

5. Pistachio Mint Pesto
  
INGREDIENTS:
  1/2 cup pistachios, roughly chopped
  1 cup fresh mint leaves
  6 cups fresh basil leaves
  1 lemon, zested and juiced
  1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  4 cloves garlic, minced
  1 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  
DIRECTIONS:
Add all ingredients to a food processor and process until
mostly smooth but some chunks remain, stopping to
scrape down the sides of the processor as needed.
Cover and refrigerate.
  
6. Marinated Chicken
  
INGREDIENTS:
  2 pounds chicken breast
  1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  2 tablespoon za’atar seasoning
  4 cloves garlic, minced
  1 teaspoon salt
  1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  
DIRECTIONS:
Mix together all ingredients except for the chicken. Add
the chicken and submerge it in the marinade. Cover and
refrigerate.
  
7. Beef Kabobs
  
INGREDIENTS:
  2 pounds oyster steak or london broil, cut into
1” pieces
  1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  1 tablespoon chili powder
  1 tablespoon garlic powder
  2 teaspoons cumin
  1/2 teaspoon salt
  1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  
DIRECTIONS:
Whisk together all ingredients except the beef. Add the
beef and toss to coat, using your hands to rub the
marinade on the meat. Cover and refrigerate.

8. Marinated Onion + Cucumber
  
INGREDIENTS:
  1 sweet onion, thinly sliced
  2 slicing cucumbers, thinly sliced in half moons
  1 cup white vinegar
  1/2 cup water
  2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  1 tablespoon honey
  1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  1/2 teaspoon salt
  2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  
DIRECTIONS:
Whisk together everything except the onion and
cucumber. Add the onion and cucumber and mix to
combine and submerge in the liquid. Cover and
refrigerate.
  
9. Tahini Yogurt Sauce
  
INGREDIENTS:
  2 cups plain greek yogurt
  1/4 cup tahini
  2 tablespoons fresh dill
  2 tablespoons fresh mint
  1 lemon, juiced
  2 cloves garlic, minced
  1/2 teaspoon salt
  1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  
DIRECTIONS:
Add all ingredients to a medium bowl and whisk to
combine. Cover and refrigerate.

 Monday Rally
Grilled Salmon w/ Tahini Yogurt Sauce,
Sauteed Asparagus, + Lemony Orzo
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
Tahini Yogurt Sauce (½) 
Sauteed Asparagus
Lemony Orzo
 
Additional Ingredients:
4, 6-8oz salmon filets (or 2 filets for 2
servings and 6 for 6 servings)
Lemon wedges to serve – optional 
DIRECTIONS :
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Place the asparagus on a
baking sheet and the orzo in an oven safe dish. Reheat for about 10-15
minutes or until warmed through, stirring the orzo halfway through.
Meanwhile, heat the grill or grill pan to medium high heat, about 450
degrees. Lightly oil the salmon filets and season with a pinch of salt and
pepper. When the grill is hot, place the salmon skin side up on the grill.
Grill for 3-6 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of the salmon,
until cooked through to desired doneness. Remove from the heat. Serve
the salmon with the tahini yogurt sauce on top and the asparagus and orzo
on the side.

 Tuesday Rally
Chicken Pitas w/ Pistachio Mint Pesto,
Marinated Cucumber and Onion, +
Crispy Baked Sweet Potato Fries
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
Marinated Chicken
Pistachio Mint Pesto (½)
Marinated Cucumber and Onion (½)
Baked Sweet Potato Fries (½)
 
Additional Ingredients:
4 pitas (or 2 for 2 servings and 6 for 6
servings)
DIRECTIONS :
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Place the sweet potato fries
on a baking sheet and reheat for about 10 minutes or until warmed
through. Broil for 1 minute if desired for extra crispiness. Meanwhile, heat
the grill or grill pan to medium high heat, about 450 degrees. When the grill
is hot, place the chicken breasts on the grill. Grill for 6-8 minutes per side,
depending on the thickness of the chicken, until cooked through. Remove
and let rest for about 10 minutes, then slice. Assemble the pitas with the
pistachio mint pesto, grilled chicken, and marinated cucumber and onion
with the baked sweet potato fries served on the side.

Beef Kabobs + Summer Corn Tomato
Orzo Salad
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
Marinated Beef
Summer Corn Tomato Orzo Salad
DIRECTIONS :
Thread the beef pieces onto skewers. Heat the grill or grill pan to medium
high heat, about 450 degrees. When the grill is hot, place the skewers on
the grill. Grill for 3-4 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of the

minutes. Serve the beef skewers with the summer corn tomato orzo salad

 Thursday Rally
Loaded Sweet Potato Fries w/ Marinated
Cucumber and Onion, Tahini Yogurt
Sauce, + Pistachio Mint Pesto
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
Baked Sweet Potato Fries (½)
Marinated Cucumber and Onion (½)
Pistachio Mint Pesto (½)
Tahini Yogurt Sauce (½) 
 
Additional Ingredients:
½ cup feta cheese, crumbled (or ¼ cup
for 2 servings and ¾ cup for 6 servings)
½ cup kalamata olives, halved  (or ¼
cup for 2 servings and ¾ cup for 6
servings)

Plate the sweet potato fries on a serving platter. Top with the marinated

cheese and kalamata olives.
