DINI'S TIPS & TRICKS
Here are some tips, tricks, and alternate serving ideas for this weeks prep!
Make this week VEGETARIAN
-Replace the spatchcock chicken with fish filets of choice and cook with lemon, oregano, and spices. Cook potatoes
separately.
-Add extra beans to the enchilada casserole or chickpeas to replace the protein from chicken. You can also you
Beyond Meat and make it a “beef" enchilada casserole.
 
Make this week more Diet Friendly
-Replace quinoa with prepared and cooked cauliflower rice in the enchilada casserole.
 
Make this week more KID FRIENDLY
-Serve the enchilada casserole in tortillas or tacos
-Serve the zucchini fritters in buns. You can also make them mini size so they're more dunkable!
-Let the kids assemble their own pizzas for a fun DIY dinner night!

Sunday Prep for the Week of 6/23/24
4 SERVINGS
1. Spatchcock Roasted Chickens
  
INGREDIENTS:
  2 whole 4-5 pound chickens, spatchcocked (or
butterflied)
  2 heads garlic, tops trimmed to expose cloves
  4 potatoes, medium dice
  1 handful fresh oregano
  1 lemon quartered
  Vegetable or extra light olive oil
  Salt and pepper
  
DIRECTIONS:
Preheat oven to 400°F. Place chickens on two baking
sheets or one extra large baking sheet. Place a head of
garlic face-down under each chicken. Squeeze the
quartered lemons over the chicken and then divide
lemons among baking trays and distribute the potatoes
around. Place the oregano under each chicken and one
or two lemons under each. Massage the chickens and
potatoes with some oil and a nice amount of salt and
pepper, being sure to season both tops and bottoms of
chicken. Roast for 70 minutes until cooked through
(165°F internal temperature if you have a meat
thermometer) and golden. Remove from oven. Once
cool, discard some lemons and squeeze garlic out of the
peels (if soft enough) and massage all over cooked
chickens. Store in the refrigerator.
  
2. Roasted Brussels Sprouts
  
INGREDIENTS:
  3 lb. Brussels sprouts, halved
  Salt, to season
  4 tablespoons oil
  
DIRECTIONS:
Preheat oven to 400°F. Place Brussels sprouts on a
baking sheet (pareve or meat) and coat in oil. Roast for
30-40 minutes until golden. Let cool and store.
  
3. Quinoa
  
INGREDIENTS:
  2 cups white quinoa
  3.5 cups water
  
DIRECTIONS:
In a medium pot (pareve) mix together quinoa and water.
Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer until the water is
absorbed, about 10 minutes. Uncover and fluff with a
fork. Set aside one cup (or 1/2 cup for 2 servings, and 1
1/2 cups for 6 servings) for the zucchini fritters (recipe
below) and store the rest for the chicken enchilada
casserole.
  
4. Sautéed Onions and Peppers
5
INGREDIENTS:
  3 tablespoons oil
  1 spanish onion, small dice
  2 red bell peppers, small dice
  Salt, to season
  2 cups canned black beans, drained and rinsed
DIRECTIONS:
In a large (meat or pareve) pan heat the oil on medium-
high heat and sauté the peppers and onions until
softened, seasoning with salt. Let cool and mix in black
beans. Store.
  
5. Zucchini Fritters
  
INGREDIENTS:
  4 medium zucchinis - shredded (with a box
grater or food processor)
  1 cup cooked quinoa (from recipe #3 above)
  3/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  1/4 cup flour (AP, whole wheat, or gluten free
alternative)
  1 egg
  Salt and pepper, to season
  Oil, for frying
  
DIRECTIONS:
Place shredded zucchini on a cheesecloth or clean
kitchen towel and wring out as much liquid as possible.
Once very dry add it to a large (dairy) bowl. Add cooked
quinoa, parmesan, flour, egg, salt and pepper. Cover and
refrigerate until ready to cook prior before serving. Once
ready to cook and prepare fresh heat 1-2 tablespoons of
oil in a large pan. Form the zucchini mixture into round
patties in the pan and fry like a latke. Allow each side to
become golden brown and then flip. If needed flip over
once more to the first time and finish cooking an
additional few minutes until fully cooked through.
  
6. Quick Enchilada Sauce
  
INGREDIENTS:
  1.5 cups marinara sauce
  3/4 teaspoons garlic powder
  1.5 teaspoons cumin
  1 teaspoon chili powder
  2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  Salt and pepper, to season
  
DIRECTIONS:
Mix all ingredients together in a (meat or pareve) storage
jar and store in the fridge.
  
7. Honey Lemon Yogurt Sauce
  
INGREDIENTS:
  1 cup plain, unsweetened Greek yogurt
  1 tablespoon honey
  1 lemon, zested and juiced
  2 tablespoons chopped chives
  
DIRECTIONS:
Whisk all ingredients together and store.

 Monday Rally
Herb and Lemon Spatchcock Chicken
with Potatoes and Garlic with Brussels
Sprouts
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
-1 whole spatchcocked chicken + 2 
breasts and 2 thighs from the second,
including all the potatoes (or 1/2 a
spatchcocked chicken + 1 breast & 1
thigh for 2 servings, and 1 1/2
spatchcocked chickens + 2 breasts + 3
thighs for 6 servings)
–Brussels sprouts (half)
DIRECTIONS :
Add half of the Brussels sprouts in with one of the spatchcocked chickens
plus the drumsticks from the other chicken, and all of the potatoes (reserve
the other chicken breasts and thighs for the enchilada). Heat covered at
300F° for 40 minutes or so, until fully warmed through. Serve family style.

 Wednesday Rally
Quinoa Enchilada Casserole with
Brussels Sprouts
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
-2 cups quinoa (or 1 cup for 2
servings, and 3 cups for 6 servings)
-Reserved 2 chicken breasts and 2 
thighs (or 1 breast + 1 thigh for 2
servings, and 3 breasts + 3 thighs for 6
servings)
–Sautéed peppers and onions
–Brussels sprouts (half)
Additional Ingredients:
-1 1/2-2 cups quick enchilada sauce
(from Sunday prep) (or 3/4-1 cup for 2
servings, and 2 1/4-3 cups for 6
servings)
-Salt and pepper, to taste
-1 avocado, sliced (or 1/2 avocado for 2
servings, and 1 1/2 avocados for 6
servings)
-Cilantro
-Chives
-Lime wedges
DIRECTIONS :
Preheat oven to 350°F. In a large (meat) skillet (or baking dish), mix
together the quinoa, chicken, sautéed peppers and onions, enchilada
sauce and season with salt and pepper. Heat in the oven uncovered for
15-20 minutes until warmed through. Top with avocado, cilantro, chives
and limes and serve alongside warmed Brussels sprouts.

 Thursday Rally
Greek Pizza with Yogurt Sauce and
Optional Side Salad (weeknight bite)
4 SERVINGS
INGREDIENTS :
-Optional leftover yogurt sauce, for
garnish
Additional Ingredients:
-2 ready made pizza crusts or dough
balls, formed into pizza rounds (or 1
crust for 2 servings, and 3 crusts for 6
servings)
-2-3 cups marinara sauce (or 1-1 1/2
cups for 2 servings, and 3-4 1/2 cups
for 6 servings)
-5-6 cups shredded mozzarella (or 2
1/2-3 cups for 2 servings, and 7 1/2-9
cups for 6 servings)
-2 cups pepperoncini (or 1 cup for 2
servings, and 3 cups for 6 servings)
-3-4 cups frozen broccoli, thawed (or 1
1/2-2 cups for 2 servings, and 4 1/2-6
cups for 6 servings)
-2 cups sliced black or green olives (or
1 cup for 2 servings, and 3 cups for 6
servings)
-Crushed red chili flakes, for garnish
-Optional side salad
DIRECTIONS :
Prepare pizzas by placing pizza rounds on baking sheets and topping with
sauce, cheese, and toppings of choice. Bake according to pizza crust
package instructions and then top with garnishes once out of the oven!
Serve with optional side salad.
